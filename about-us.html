About Us

Welcome to Lingaraj Appa Engineering College!

Lingaraj Appa Engineering College operates under the esteemed Sharanabasaveshwar Vidya Vardhak Sangha (SVVS), Kalaburagi. Established in 1903, the Sangha is named after the revered mystic saint of Deccan India, Lord Sharanabasaveshwar.

At the heart of the Sangha lies the guiding principle: "No religion is greater than service" and "Service to humanity is service to God."

This philosophy, known as Kayaka and Dasoha, is a legacy left behind by Lord Sharanabasaveshwar, upheld to this day by the Peethadhipathis of the Sharanabasaveshwar Samsthan.

Our Rich Heritage

The Sharanabasaveshwar Samsthan, also referred to as the Sharanabasaveshwar Mahadasoha Peetha, boasts a vibrant spiritual, cultural, social, and educational history. This legacy represents the glorious heritage of our nation, deeply rooted in the principles of service and humanity.

Philosophy of Dasoha

The philosophy of Dasoha has been the cornerstone of our mission, inspiring selfless service and educational excellence. Guided by this principle, the 7th Mahadasoha Peethadhipathi of the Sharanabasaveshwar Samsthan, Gulbarga, founded the Sharanabasaveshwar Vidya Vardhak Sangha to serve the community.

At Lingaraj Appa Engineering College, we strive to honor this tradition by fostering academic excellence, holistic development, and service to humanity.
